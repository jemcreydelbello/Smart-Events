What It Does:
  ✓ Set maximum participants per event
  ✓ Track registrations against capacity
  ✓ Calculate available spots automatically
  ✓ Display on event cards

Capacity Calculation:
  Available = Total Capacity - Current Registrations
  
  Example:
    Event: "Tech Summit"
    Capacity: 100
    Registrations: 35
    Available: 65
    Display: "Capacity: 100 | Available: 65"
